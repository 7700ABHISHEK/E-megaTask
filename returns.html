Return Requirements

  • Items must be unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging and tags must be intact
  • Proof of purchase (order number or receipt)

How to Return an Item

1

Initiate Return

Log into your account and go to Order History. Select the item you want to return.

2

Print Return Label

We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label. Print it and attach to your package.

3

Ship It Back

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location.

4

Get Your Refund

Once we receive your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.
